Artist Summary

Luis Tapia is a visual artist based in Lake Worth Beach, Florida. His current practice focuses on original paintings and mixed-media concepts inspired by South Florida life, including music, coastal culture, restaurants, cars, small businesses, public spaces, and everyday visual identity.

His creative background combines early arts training, digital design, and hands-on fabrication. Luis approaches artwork with both an artist’s eye and a builder’s mindset, considering composition, color, surface, presentation, and how each work interacts with the space where it is displayed.
